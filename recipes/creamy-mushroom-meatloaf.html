Creamy Mushroom Meatloaf

Meatloaf but with a different take, this recipe involves flavours from associated with salisbury steak. It combines the savory flavours of mushrooms with that all too good beef broth that we all love. This is by no means a quick dish but trust me it will be worth the wait.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2 cups shiitake mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 sprig fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 1/2 cups beef broth
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 (2 1/2 pound) uncooked prepared beef, veal and pork meatloaf
